Three Olive

Location: Wood Green, London

Price Range: ££

Summary: A cozy café in Wood Green with a fresh, green vibe and great coffee.

Review

Three Olive is the kind of spot that feels like a little hidden gem. It’s filled with plants, sunlight, and friendly faces. The coffee is rich, the pastries are flaky and fresh, and there’s a calmness to the atmosphere that makes it perfect for reading, journaling, or chatting with a friend. Definitely one of FiFi’s top café picks in North London!
